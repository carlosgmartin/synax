.. py:class:: synax.Residual(module: Module)

   Residual map.

   Computes

   .. math::
       y = x + f(x)

   where :math:`f` is a given module.

   References:

   - *Deep residual learning for image recognition*. 2015.
     https://arxiv.org/abs/1512.03385.

   :param module: Module to apply.
