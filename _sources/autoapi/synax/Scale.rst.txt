.. py:class:: synax.Scale(dimension: int, initializer: Initializer = nn.initializers.ones, regularizer: synax._regularizers.Regularizer = zero)



   Elementwise scaling.

   Computes

   .. math::
       y = x \odot a

   where :math:`a` is a learned vector.

   :param dimension: Input dimension.
   :param initializer: Initializer.
   :param regularizer: Regularizer.
